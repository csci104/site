Course Staff and Hours

All questions related to the course must use the course Piazza page. For general or assignment questions, this allows other students to see your question and answer. For individual or administrative questions, posting to the discussion board privately will get "more eyes on it" so that hopefully it can be answered quickly. Course staff will tag the instructor or course administrator, if necessary, to bring prompt attention to your query.

Note that we may change the listed office hours as needed, or add more before homework deadlines or examinations.
